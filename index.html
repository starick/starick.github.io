Welcome! I'm Jakob, an upcoming 3D artist currently located in the area of Berlin, Germany. Creativity has been my driving passion since my first years. While other children wanted to be policemen and doctors, I wanted to become an author. While the direction itself changed, the will to create worlds, tell stories and inspire people with my own hands' work has stayed a constant. When I first delved into 3D art in 2011 I knew this was the one thing I want to do for the rest of my life.

I combine a wide range of interests, ranging from technical to artistical topics. My work is the synthesis of all my passion, my technical background, my sense of art and the experiences I had the great joy to make so far. I am always willing to learn, to broaden my horizon and to find myself as a part of new, interesting projects.
